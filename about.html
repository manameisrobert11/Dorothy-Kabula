About the Foundation

Legal status, background, leadership, vision, mission, and values.

Legal status

The Dorothy Kabula Welfare Foundation is a Non-Profit Making Association Incorporated under Section 21 and registered in accordance with the laws and governance framework of the Republic of Namibia.

Background

The Foundation was established to promote unity, dignity, and sustainable socio-economic development within the Zambezi Region through community mobilisation and partnerships.

Founder and Chairperson

Hon. Dorothy Mareka Kabula
Founder and Chairperson of the Foundation and the first female Governor of the Zambezi Region.

Vision

A united, empowered, and economically thriving Zambezi Region where all communities enjoy sustainable livelihoods, wellbeing, and equal opportunity.

Mission

To mobilise partnerships, resources, and participation that advance social welfare, entrepreneurship, agriculture, empowerment, culture, and inclusive growth.

Core values

Unity & Inclusion Community Empowerment Integrity & Accountability Sustainability & Impact Partnership & Collaboration Respect for Culture & Heritage

Community-led development in the Zambezi Region.
